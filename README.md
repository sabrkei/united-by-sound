# United By Sound - UX UI Module

United by Sound 🎵
A festival website and online merch store for United by Sound — a music festival experience designed to bring people together through live music, culture, and community.

Project Overview
United by Sound is a multi-page website that serves as the central hub for festival-goers. It provides everything attendees need before, during, and after the event — from lineup and schedules to food info, accessibility details, and official festival merchandise.
The site is split into two main experiences:

Festival Info Site — all the essential information about the event
Merch Store — an online shop for official United by Sound apparel and accessories


Site Structure
🎤 Main Festival Site
Navigation
The top navbar includes quick links to: Tickets, Music, News, Info, Shop, and a search icon.
Hero Section
A full-width banner image of the festival with the United by Sound logo, immediately setting the atmosphere for the site.
Music
Showcases the festival's musical offerings across three cards:

Program — the full festival schedule and stage lineup
Artists — profiles and information on performing acts
After Works / NYE — special late-night and new year's eve events

Info
Six informational sections covering everything attendees need to know:

Info — general festival information
About — the story and mission behind United by Sound
Contact — how to get in touch with the organizers
FAQ — answers to common questions
Food — food vendors and dining options at the festival
Accessibility — information for attendees with accessibility needs

News
A three-column grid of the latest festival news and announcements, each with an image and article preview.
Footer
Social media links (X / Twitter, Instagram) and legal/contact information.

🛍️ Merch Store
Navigation
The store has its own dedicated navbar with links to: What's New, Apparel, Accessories, search, and a cart icon.
What's New
A hero banner featuring a model wearing festival merchandise, with a curated selection of newly added items displayed below.
Apparel
A product grid showcasing official United by Sound clothing:

T-shirt — available in multiple colors with size selector
Crewneck — available in multiple colors with size selector
Knit hoodie — available in multiple colors with size selector
Sweat-shirt — available in multiple colors with size selector

Each product card includes a photo, name, price, color swatches, size options, and an Add to cart button.
Accessories
A product grid for festival accessories:

Tygväska (tote bag)
Snapback (cap)

Same card format as apparel — photo, price, and Add to cart button.
Footer
Social links and standard site information, consistent with the main festival site.

Design
The site uses a bold, dark aesthetic with high-contrast imagery that reflects the energy of a live music festival. Key design choices include:

Dark backgrounds with vibrant photography
Clear typographic hierarchy for easy scanning
A consistent card-based layout for music, info, and products
Color swatches and size selectors inline on product cards for a smooth shopping experience
